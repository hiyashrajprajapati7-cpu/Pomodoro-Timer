Why We Built This Timer

We were tired of productivity tools cluttered with noisy advertising, forced login panels, paywalled premium features, and intrusive telemetry trackers. Focus shouldn't require registration. We designed this free pomodoro timer to provide direct, clean, and instant access to top-tier cognitive focus tools.

🔬

The Science of Sound

Instead of ordinary ticking clocks, our app features an advanced multi-channel binaural beats synthesizer. By playing different sound frequencies in each ear, it guides your brain waves into targeted states: 40Hz Gamma waves for analytical deep study, or 10Hz Alpha waves for peaceful reflection.

Features At A Glance

This aesthetic pomodoro timer app features local session stats tracking, beautiful visual progress interfaces, customizable intervals, keyboard shortcuts reference, integrated Spotify embeds, and our highly acclaimed responsive sound mixer. Everything runs 100% locally on your computer for maximum performance and complete privacy.

Whether you are using it as an online pomodoro timer for study, writing, coding, or organizing tasks inside your Notion workspace, we invite you to settle in, adjust your nature ambient mixes, and enjoy effortless deep cognitive flow.
