Absolute Security

Your data belongs to you. Zero tracking.

We value your digital privacy. Unlike most other tools, our pomodoro timer app does not collect, analyze, transmit, or monetize your activity logs. Settle in with complete peace of mind.

🔒

No Accounts

No sign-up, email, password, or third-party login flows are integrated. You stay entirely anonymous.

🗄️

Local Storage

Your configurations, task lists, and history streaks are stored 100% locally in your personal browser container (localStorage).

🛡️

Zero Cookies

We do not use tracking pixels, persistent cookie jars, or advertising networks. Your browser cache belongs to you.

1. Local Configuration and Persistent Data

When using our online pomodoro timer, settings (such as active theme, custom timer durations, and sound selections) and checklists are cached in your local web browser using standard client-side sandbox APIs. These variables remain entirely on your physical machine and can be completely wiped at any time by clearing your browser cache.

2. Analytics & Infrastructure Logs

We prioritize extreme minimalist hosting. Standard web containers may log basic server-side networking parameters (such as generalized IP routing paths and response statuses) to maintain application runtime availability, but these parameters are not aggregated or coupled with custom user activity patterns.

3. Third-Party Web Embeds

Our application features optional integrated modules like Spotify ambient tracks. Engaging with these dynamic embeds routes standard connection parameters directly through Spotify's infrastructure, which is governed by their respective privacy terms. If you do not actively load or click these embeds, no data is exchanged.

4. Contact & User Support

If you actively submit support feedback or feature requests through our contact portal, the information you provide (name, email, subject, message) is processed securely to help address your queries. We never bundle, rent, or sell your correspondence logs.
